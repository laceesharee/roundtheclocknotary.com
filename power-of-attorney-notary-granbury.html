Power of Attorney Notary Granbury — Available 24/7

Round The Clock Notary provides mobile notary service for power of attorney documents throughout Granbury and Hood County — available 24/7, including evenings, weekends, and hospital bedside visits.

Types of Power of Attorney We Notarize

Durable power of attorney, medical power of attorney, financial power of attorney, limited power of attorney, springing power of attorney, and general power of attorney documents.

What to Know Before Your Appointment

All signers must be present with valid government-issued photo ID. Documents must be complete — we cannot notarize documents with blank spaces. The signer must appear willing and mentally aware at the time of signing.

Hospital and Bedside Power of Attorney

We provide urgent bedside notary service for medical power of attorney at Lake Granbury Medical Center and all care facilities in Hood County. Call 817-512-7217 any time for same-hour emergency service.
